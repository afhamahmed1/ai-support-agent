## Product overview
Acme Analytics is a product-analytics platform for SaaS teams. Track events, build funnels, monitor retention, and share dashboards with your team.

## Pricing
- **Free** — up to 10,000 events/month, 1 project, 30-day data retention.
- **Pro — $49/month** — up to 1,000,000 events/month, unlimited projects, 12-month retention, funnels and retention reports.
- **Business — $199/month** — up to 10,000,000 events/month, role-based access control, SSO (SAML), and priority support.
- **Enterprise** — custom volume, SLA, and dedicated support. Contact sales.

Annual billing saves 20%. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time; changes are prorated.

## Free trial
Every paid plan includes a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start. If you don't upgrade, your account automatically moves to the Free plan when the trial ends.

## Integrations
Acme integrates with Segment, Shopify, Stripe, and Webhooks, plus a REST API. SDKs are available for JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, and iOS/Android.

## Getting started
1. Create a project in the dashboard.
2. Install the SDK or connect a source (e.g., Segment).
3. Send your first event with `acme.track('Signed Up')`.
4. Build a dashboard from the Events tab.

## Exporting your data
Export raw events as CSV or JSON from **Settings → Data → Export**. Business and Enterprise plans can schedule automated exports to Amazon S3 or Google Cloud Storage.

## Resetting your password
On the login page, click **Forgot password**. We email a reset link valid for 60 minutes. If it doesn't arrive, check spam or contact support.

## Cancellation and refunds
Cancel anytime from **Settings → Billing**. Your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on the first payment of any annual plan.

## Support response times
- Free and Pro: email support, responses within 2 business days.
- Business: priority email, responses within 4 business hours.
- Enterprise: dedicated Slack channel and a named account manager.
